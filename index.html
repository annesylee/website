I am a Master's student at Cornell University in the department of Information Science. I completed my B.A. in Communication at Cornell University. Also, I completed my M.A. in Communication at Seoul National University . I worked at Robotics and Media Institute as a researcher at the Korea Institute of Science and Technology .

My research interests are in human-computer interaction (HCI), Computer-Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW), and mobile communication. Esepcially, I am interested in solving diverse social phenomena such as privacy, bias, online segregation, and miscommunication with new technologies in diverse contexts. Also, i am interested in analyzing and observing interactive and affective interactions between users and diverse media platforms.

I love nature because it give time for myself and a chance to relax. My favorite place is the crystal-blue sea with clear waters and sand.
